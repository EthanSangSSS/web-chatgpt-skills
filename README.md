# Web ChatGPT Skills

Single-file, Web ChatGPT-oriented skills. Every package is self-contained at:

```text
upload-packages/<skill>/SKILL.md
```

Upload a selected `SKILL.md` to ChatGPT as a skill file. These packages are designed for conversations, uploaded material, and optional web search; they do not assume a local terminal, filesystem, MCP server, credentials, or background agent.

## Software and architecture

- `architecture-diagram` — creates readable SVG, HTML, or Mermaid system diagrams.
- `systematic-debugging` — evidence-led debugging with hypotheses and discriminating tests.
- `test-driven-development` — test design and RED-GREEN-REFACTOR with user-supplied test evidence.

## Product and project management

- `requirement-engineering` — turns ambiguity into testable requirements.
- `kanban-orchestrator` — decomposes an outcome into dependency-aware work packages.

## Research and perspective modeling

- `customer-research` — synthesizes interviews, feedback, and web evidence.
- `research-paper-writing` — plans and reviews technical research manuscripts.
- `nuwa` — builds evidence-bounded perspective skills without deceptive impersonation.

## Visual and creative design

- `baoyu-article-illustrator` — plans article visuals and image prompts.
- `baoyu-comic` — creates educational comic storyboards.
- `baoyu-infographic` — creates evidence-aware infographic specifications.

## Writing, safety, and quality

- `content-polishing-workflow` — improves clarity, voice, structure, and evidence fidelity.
- `skill-vetter` — security-first review for skills and workflows.
- `industrial-self-audit-protocol` — evidence-based final quality gate.

## Important limits

- A skill cannot execute commands, run tests, access local files, or connect to a third-party service unless that capability is separately available in ChatGPT.
- Do not upload secrets, private source code, personally identifiable information, or credentials.
- Generated diagrams, images, tests, and analyses must be reviewed by a human before production use.
- `nuwa` produces an advisor-style reasoning framework, not a claim to be or impersonate a real person.

## Status

These are portable adaptations of local workflows for Web ChatGPT. They intentionally favor explicit evidence, user confirmation, and clear uncertainty over autonomous execution.
