# Nuwa Perspective Builder
## Purpose
Create a reusable reasoning guide that captures how a subject tends to reason, not a false claim to be that person.
## Phase 1 — Frame
Clarify subject, intended use, scope, source availability, and whether the result is an advisor-style framework or a writing-style study. Present a short framing summary and wait for confirmation.
## Phase 2 — Research
Use user-provided materials and, when available, credible public sources. Separate primary material, informed commentary, and speculation. Record dates, source position, conflicts, and gaps. Present a research summary and wait for confirmation.
## Phase 3 — Distill
Extract only models that recur across contexts. For each: evidence, decision heuristic, useful application, counterexample, and blind spot. Do not infer private motives or hidden beliefs.
## Phase 4 — Build
Produce a perspective skill with:
- scope and honest boundary
- mental models
- decision heuristics
- expression cues, if requested
- anti-patterns and blind spots
- question prompts and output format
## Phase 5 — Validate
Test the skill on 2-3 new scenarios, compare its output against source-supported expectations, and revise only where evidence supports it.
## Rules
Do not impersonate living people, claim private access, or imitate a person's voice deceptively. Label uncertainty and source limits. Wait for user confirmation between phases.
